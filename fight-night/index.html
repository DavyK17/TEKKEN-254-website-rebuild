Savanna Fight Night

The second competition on TEKKEN 254's portfolio, Savanna Fight Night (SFN) is a lineal championship series introduced to maintain a competitive spirit within and foster rivalries amongst players in the community. The series is also a replacement for the Hall of Pain (HoP), which remained largely inactive since its inception in 2018. All SFN fight cards are streamed every second and fourth Friday and Saturday of the month on our Twitch and YouTube channels from 7 to 9 pm East African Time.

The series uses a lineal ranking system akin to those used in combat sports such as boxing, with only the top 5 ranked players eligible to fight on a main event. Each SFN fight card consists of 3 undercard fights (FT3) and the main event (FT5).

Players may issue challenges to other players at any rank, with community voting ultimately deciding which challenges will feature on a SFN fight card. Registration for SFN is completely free and open to EAC residents of all skill levels aged 13 and above.
